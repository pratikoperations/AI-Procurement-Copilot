# Perplexity Method Validation Prompt

Act as an evidence-based procurement methodology reviewer. Use current, reputable sources and provide citations with publication dates.

Validate whether the project terminology and general methodology are defensible for:

- Total cost of ownership
- Should-cost modeling
- Supplier-risk assessment
- Supplier scorecards
- SRM segmentation and governance
- Supplier financial due diligence wording
- ESG terminology and evidence requirements
- Allocation and continuity principles
- Data-quality governance
- Decision explainability
- Human approval and model-risk controls

For each topic provide:

1. Widely accepted principles
2. Practices that vary by industry or company
3. Claims in the project that are defensible
4. Claims that need qualification
5. Missing controls or caveats
6. Recommended wording changes
7. Source citations and dates
8. Severity: Critical, Major, Moderate, Minor or Enhancement

Do not treat generic industry guidance as proof that the project's exact weights, thresholds or formulas are correct. Explicitly separate established practice from context-dependent policy choices.
