## Stable Scope

The platform includes category-aware RFQ analysis, should-cost, TCO, risk, Procurement Intelligence, Supplier Intelligence, validation assurance, evidence-governed Supplier 360 outputs, category-aware communications, scenario stress testing, negotiation intelligence, and separate business-readable and audit exports.

## Release Integrity

- Original and normalized quotation data are separated.
- Currency and unit consistency are governed.
- PET Resin uses kg.
- RFQ and governed Supplier 360 scores are explicitly differentiated.
- Financial, ESG, and innovation conclusions are evidence-governed.
- Unsupported recommendation roles are withheld.
- Risk Resilience Score terminology is used.
- Freight stress changes unit and annual TCO.
- Scenario rendering supports governed and legacy schema headings.
- Readable and machine-readable exports are separated.
- Human approval remains mandatory.
