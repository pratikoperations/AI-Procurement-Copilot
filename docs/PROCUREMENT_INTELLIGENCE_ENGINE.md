# Procurement Intelligence Engine

## Purpose

Build 0.9.3 transforms AI Procurement Copilot from analytics into deterministic, explainable decision support.

## Components

- Executive decision engine
- Procurement strategy engine
- Supplier allocation optimizer
- Negotiation intelligence
- Procurement risk intelligence
- Scenario simulation
- AI Explainability 2.0
- Executive decision narrative

## Decision Principles

The recommendation does not rely on lowest price alone. It considers:

- Risk-adjusted TCO
- Supplier risk
- Performance
- ESG
- Lead time
- MOQ
- Payment terms
- Capacity
- Business continuity

## Strategy Outputs

- Single Source
- Dual Source
- Competitive Tender
- Strategic Partnership
- Long-Term Agreement

The engine selects a strategy using supplier count, score gap, risk, capacity, annual volume, and performance.

## Allocation Logic

Supported splits:

- 100/0
- 90/10
- 80/20
- 70/30
- 60/40
- 50/50

The optimizer uses score differentiation, capacity, risk, and performance while retaining continuity protection.

## Risk Intelligence

Risk categories include:

- Supply concentration
- Logistics
- Capacity
- ESG
- Quality
- Currency
- Country
- Geographic
- Financial

Every risk includes severity, evidence, and mitigation.

## Scenario Library

- Base Case
- Price Increase
- Lead-Time Increase
- MOQ Increase
- Capacity Reduction
- Currency Fluctuation
- Freight Increase
- ESG Penalty

Scenarios recalculate supplier scoring, allocation, recommendation, and confidence.

## Explainability Governance

Every recommendation includes:

- Why the selected supplier won
- Why alternatives did not win
- Most influential factors
- Trade-offs
- Assumptions
- Confidence

The system is transparent, rule-guided, auditable, and not black-box AI.
