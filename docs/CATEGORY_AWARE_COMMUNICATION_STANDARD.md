# Category-Aware Communication Standard

## Packaging Procurement

Supplier communications may address:

- Substrate and material specification
- Printing and conversion
- Tooling, plates, or cylinders
- Scrap and wastage
- Freight, MOQ, payment, lead time, service, recyclability, PCR, EPR, and quality documentation

## Raw Material Procurement

Supplier communications should address:

- Commodity index and reset frequency
- Producer or conversion premium
- Grade and quality specification
- Freight, duty, FX, and landed cost
- Volume rebate and payment terms
- Capacity allocation and supply assurance
- Lead time, certificate of analysis, regulatory evidence, and contingency supply

## PET Resin

Use kg and include, where relevant:

- Resin grade
- Intrinsic viscosity
- Approved application
- Index-linked formula
- Producer premium
- Supply allocation
- Certificate of analysis and compliance evidence

## Validation status language

- Blocked: evaluation paused; clarification only; no selection implication
- Insufficient Data: provisional evaluation; missing information requested
- Human Review Required: internal review pending
- Eligible With Conditions: conditions stated explicitly
- Eligible: commercial-alignment language permitted; human approval still mandatory
