# Seven-Minute Interview Demo Script

## 0:00–0:45 — Business Problem

Procurement teams often compare supplier quotes using price alone, even though true value also depends on MOQ, freight, payment terms, lead time, quality, ESG, service, working capital, and continuity risk.

## 0:45–1:30 — Product Overview

AI Procurement Copilot converts packaging RFQ data into a transparent, auditable sourcing recommendation. It supports built-in demo data and CSV/Excel uploads.

## 1:30–2:30 — Decision Summary

Show:

- Recommended supplier
- Lowest quoted supplier
- Best-value logic
- Recommendation confidence
- Executive value metrics

Explain that the tool separates quoted price from risk-adjusted value.

## 2:30–3:30 — Should-Cost and TCO

Show the packaging cost build-up and the supplier TCO breakdown. Explain how freight, inventory, working capital, lead time, and risk affect the real economic outcome.

## 3:30–4:30 — Supplier Risk and Allocation

Show supplier risk, ESG, performance, and allocation. Explain that the system avoids overconcentration and maintains a backup supplier where appropriate.

## 4:30–5:15 — Stress Testing

Run raw-material, freight, demand, and combined stress scenarios. Explain how procurement can test whether a recommendation remains resilient.

## 5:15–6:00 — Negotiation

Show the negotiation simulator and playbook. Explain target price, walk-away position, BATNA, and give/get matrix.

## 6:00–6:40 — Executive Outputs

Show:

- Executive sourcing memo
- Supplier clarification email
- Explainability panel

Explain that AI assists communication while decision rules remain visible and procurement-controlled.

## 6:40–7:00 — Close

“This project demonstrates how I combine procurement expertise, packaging intelligence, analytics, AI-enabled communication, and transparent governance to improve sourcing decisions.”
