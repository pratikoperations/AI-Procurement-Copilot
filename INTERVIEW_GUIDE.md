# Interview Guide

## Project Positioning

AI Procurement Copilot is a portfolio project demonstrating how AI-ready procurement decision intelligence can improve sourcing quality, decision transparency, risk visibility, and executive communication.

## Interview Pitch

This project converts RFQ and supplier data into a structured sourcing recommendation. It evaluates quoted price, packaging should-cost, advanced TCO, risk, ESG, supplier performance, MOQ, lead time, payment terms, and allocation constraints.

The system does not simply select the lowest price. It separates quoted price from best-value procurement decision-making.

## Key Talking Points

- Built as a transparent rule-guided AI-ready copilot.
- Award logic is explainable and auditable.
- AI supports drafting, explanation, and summarization rather than black-box decisioning.
- Packaging-specific logic is visible through the category engine.
- Architecture is extensible to raw materials and other procurement categories.
- GitHub is used as the canonical project record.

## Business Value

- Improves sourcing decision quality.
- Reduces over-reliance on lowest-price awards.
- Makes risk and TCO assumptions visible.
- Creates executive-ready sourcing recommendations.
- Supports negotiation planning.
- Enables category-specific procurement analytics.

## Career Relevance

This project supports positioning for roles in:

- Procurement transformation
- Strategic sourcing
- Packaging procurement
- Category management
- Procurement analytics
- AI-enabled operations
- Digital procurement leadership
