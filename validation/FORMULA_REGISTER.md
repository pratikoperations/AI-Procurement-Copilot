# Formula Register

## Governance

This register records the implemented calculation logic. Values are portfolio assumptions unless supported by verified source data. Every output remains subject to human review.

| Engine | Output | Formula / rule | Inputs | Units | Defaults | Valid range / boundary | Business rationale | Limitation | Release risk if wrong |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Packaging should-cost | Target unit cost | Sum of paper/substrate, resin/film/foil, ink/coating, adhesive, printing, conversion, tooling amortization, scrap, freight and margin components | Component assumptions, material and freight shocks | USD/unit | Engine baselines | Must be >0; negative shocks cannot create negative component cost | Creates transparent negotiation anchor | Not a supplier quotation or live market index | Major |
| Packaging TCO | Adjusted TCO/unit | Scenario price + freight + inventory + working-capital impact + risk penalty + lead-time buffer | Quote, volume, MOQ, lead time, payment, incoterm, risk | USD/unit | Cost of capital and carrying-rate assumptions | Quote and volume >0 | Separates quote from delivered economic cost | Simplified expected-value assumptions | Critical |
| Raw-material should-cost | Target unit cost | Commodity index + producer/conversion premium + freight + duty/import cost + quality premium + supplier margin, adjusted by shocks | Commodity, freight and FX assumptions | USD/kg or category UOM | Commodity baselines | Commodity component and total >0 | Reflects index-led sourcing economics | Static portfolio baselines; not live market data | Major |
| Raw-material TCO | Adjusted TCO/unit | Scenario commodity price + freight + duty + inventory + working capital + risk penalty + volatility buffer | Quote, volume, MOQ, lead time, payment, duty, volatility, risk | USD/UOM | Carrying rate 18%; cost of capital 12% | All cost inputs non-negative; volume >0 | Makes import, volatility and financing exposure visible | Simplified hedging and tax treatment | Critical |
| Risk score | Supplier risk score | 100 minus explicit rule penalties, clipped 0–100 | Payment, incoterm, lead time, MOQ, service, quality or commodity risk fields | Score | Documented fallback values | 0–100 | Enables deterministic comparison | Rule weights require category-owner calibration | Major |
| TCO score | Relative cost score | Minimum positive adjusted TCO / supplier adjusted TCO × 100 | Adjusted TCO | Score | None | 0–100; positive TCO required | Normalizes commercial competitiveness | Relative to current bid set only | Major |
| MOQ score | Relative MOQ score | Minimum positive MOQ / supplier MOQ × 100 | MOQ | Score | None | 0–100; MOQ non-negative | Rewards flexibility | Does not model order-frequency complexity fully | Moderate |
| Lead-time score | Relative lead score | Minimum positive lead time / supplier lead time × 100 | Lead time | Score | None | 0–100; lead time non-negative | Rewards responsiveness | Does not replace safety-stock design | Moderate |
| Payment score | Terms score | Extracted payment days, capped at 90, divided by 90 × 100 | Payment terms text | Score | 30 days when no number is found | 0–100 | Approximates working-capital benefit | Complex terms need manual interpretation | Moderate |
| Total score | Category weighted score | Weighted sum of TCO, risk, lead, payment, MOQ, performance and ESG scores | Component scores and category weights | Score | Packaging or raw-material weights | 0–100 | Creates explainable ranking | Weights are policy choices, not universal truth | Critical |
| Award confidence | Decision separation indicator | Bounded function of top-two score gap | Ranked total scores | Score | 60 when only one supplier | 55–95 in current implementation | Shows relative decisiveness | Not statistical probability or forecast accuracy | Major |
| Allocation | Recommended share | Governed split selected from 100/0 to 50/50 using score gap, capacity, risk and performance | Scores, capacity, annual volume | Percent | Capacity defaults may exist in demo data | Total exactly 100%; share must not exceed capacity | Balances value and continuity | Simplified two-supplier optimizer | Critical |
| Negotiation target | Target price | Lower of quote ×97% and bounded reference between quote ×90% and should-cost/market low | Quote, should-cost, lowest quote | USD/unit | None | Target ≤ quote; walk-away positive | Creates structured negotiation range | Not a market-clearing price | Major |
| Data confidence | Completeness score | Actual supplied % − 0.35×defaulted % − 0.20×inferred % − 0.80×missing critical %; clipped 0–100 | Critical and important fields, defaults and inferences | Score | Explicit field lists | 0–100 | Makes data quality visible | Not recommendation correctness probability | Critical |
| Supplier 360 | Overall score | Weighted performance, risk, financial indicator, ESG, innovation and strategic index | Supplier intelligence outputs | Score | Transparent profile defaults | 0–100 | Supports relationship-level comparison | Quality depends on real historical data | Major |
| Financial health | Stability indicator | 100 minus utilization, dependency, concentration and payment-stress penalties | Available supplier indicators | Score | Transparent estimates | 0–100 | Triggers due diligence | Not audited analysis or bankruptcy probability | Critical |
| ESG maturity | ESG score | Weighted environmental, social and governance dimensions | ESG evidence fields | Score | Transparent defaults | 0–100 | Structures ESG review | Requires verified documentation | Major |
| Innovation maturity | Innovation score | Average of 11 capability dimensions, clipped 0–100 | Design, automation, digital, AI and improvement fields | Score | Transparent defaults | 0–100 | Supports supplier-development agenda | Subjective without verified evidence | Moderate |
