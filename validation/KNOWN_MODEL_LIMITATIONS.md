# Known Model Limitations

## Intended maturity

AI Procurement Copilot is a portfolio-grade decision-support system. It is not a production source-to-contract platform and does not connect to verified supplier masters, live commodity indices, ERP, QMS, contract, financial, sanctions, ESG or country-risk data.

## Key limitations

1. **Static baselines:** Packaging and raw-material should-cost assumptions are illustrative and require category-owner calibration.
2. **Simplified TCO:** Tax, hedging, inventory policy, warehousing, obsolescence, tooling ownership, rebates, indexation and contractual mechanisms are simplified.
3. **Relative scoring:** Several scores are relative to the current bid set and may change when suppliers are added or removed.
4. **Policy weights:** Scoring weights and thresholds are governance choices, not universally correct procurement standards.
5. **Capacity evidence:** Demo capacity may be defaulted or synthetic. Allocation is not production-feasible until verified.
6. **Financial indicators:** Financial outputs are signals based on available fields, not audited financial analysis, credit ratings or bankruptcy probability.
7. **ESG evidence:** ESG maturity requires verified certifications, policies, audit results and emissions data.
8. **Innovation scoring:** Innovation maturity is subjective without demonstrated projects, evidence and business outcomes.
9. **Country and logistics risk:** No live geopolitical, sanctions, port, weather or transport data is used.
10. **Supplier identity:** Similar names, parent-company relationships and site-level distinctions require master-data governance.
11. **Currency and UOM:** Mixed currency and unit comparisons are blocked unless normalized; automatic conversion is intentionally avoided.
12. **Recommendation confidence:** Award confidence indicates model separation, not statistical accuracy or probability of success.
13. **Data-confidence score:** Measures completeness and reliance on defaults/inferences, not truthfulness or correctness.
14. **Scenario analysis:** Scenarios are deterministic sensitivity tests, not forecasts.
15. **Human approval:** Commercial, legal, quality, finance, compliance, sustainability and executive review remains mandatory.

## Prohibited claims

Do not claim that the system autonomously selects suppliers, predicts bankruptcy, certifies ESG compliance, guarantees savings, replaces due diligence or is production-ready without verified integrations and control testing.
